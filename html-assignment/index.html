About Me

I am Gauldwin and here are a few things you might or might not be interested in knowing about myself. It might sound really weird or random but I've always related a lot to water. Yup -- water. Water is fluid, adaptable and able to fit into any space it enters, and I see myself the same way. I enter spaces and situations without losing my sense of self...or atleast I try haha.

"Stay Lost, It's Fun." - BlurryMvmnt
  • I enjoy listening to music and dancing as a way to freely express myself and stay active.
  • On my leisure time, I love going to random places while dressing up and doing funshoots.

This tagline represents the idea that I want to put out there through my personal brand called the, BlurryMvmnt. It represents how I approach life -- with curiosity, openness, and willingness to embrace uncertainty. Like water, I don't limit myself to a fixed destination but instead, I allow life to just do its own thing. Being "Lost" is not always a bad thing in my opinion. Sometimes it just means being present and open to wherever life flows next.
